Methodology

How the regime is read, and how capital follows.

i The framework

Parthsarthi reads the Indian market regime through eight components, each scored against historical bands calibrated on multi-decade data. The components sum to 100 points. The total score maps to one of six regime bands, and each band dictates a target equity allocation.

The system is built to remove emotion from allocation decisions. It does not predict direction. It reads the conditions and recommends a posture.

ii The eight components

C1
Valuation
22 points · Nifty PE, MCap/GDP, earnings yield gap
How expensive is the market versus its earnings, the economy, and bond yields?
C2
Credit & Rates
14 points · AAA spread, credit growth, yield curve
Are financial conditions easy or tightening? Credit is the oxygen of equity returns.
C3
Trend & Breadth
13 points · Nifty vs 200DMA, % stocks above 200DMA
Is the market trending up with broad participation, or narrowly led?
C4
Volatility
10 points · India VIX percentile, VIX vs 30-day average
Is fear elevated? High VIX is both a risk signal and an opportunity signal.
C5
Flows
12 points · FII, DII, SIP YoY
Who is buying, who is selling. Domestic SIP flows are the structural anchor.
C6
Macro India
12 points · RBI stance, CPI, PMI, GST
The state of the underlying economy. Macro leads earnings; earnings lead markets.
C7
Global Cross-Asset
12 points · US 10Y, DXY, US VIX, INR, gold
India does not trade in isolation. Global risk appetite sets the tide.
C8
Crude
5 points · Brent versus 6-month average
India is a net oil importer. Cheap crude is a tailwind, expensive crude is a tax.

iii The six regime bands

The total score maps to a target equity allocation. The remaining capital is split between debt (75%) and gold (25%).

75 – 100 Full Deploy 85% equity
60 – 74 Aggressive 70% equity
45 – 59 Active 55% equity
35 – 44 Cautious 40% equity
25 – 34 Freeze 25% equity
0 – 24 Exit All 10% equity

iv Safety override

A single hardcoded safety rule sits above the band logic.

If Nifty TTM PE exceeds 26, equity allocation is capped at 70% regardless of regime.

Bubble valuations have historically preceded the deepest drawdowns. This override exists to refuse maximum exposure when the math is screaming — even when the regime score looks constructive.

v What the system does not do

This is a regime-allocation framework. It does not pick individual stocks, time entries and exits, or trade derivatives. Stock selection is handled by separate engines (Momentum Hunter, Value Warriors, Compounders, Fortress) that operate within the equity allocation Engine A sets.

The system runs on a weekly cadence. It does not react to intraday noise.
